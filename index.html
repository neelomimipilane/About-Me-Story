About Me

Every line of code has a beginning. Mine started with curiosity.

I am Mimi, a 21 year old Computer Systems Engineering student from Mochudi, Botswana, a software engineer in training, and someone who believes that technology is more than just machines and algorithms.

My journey into technology began with a desire to understand how ideas become reality. What started as curiosity grew into a passion for building software, solving problems, and creating solutions that make an impact.

Beyond coding, I believe technology is about people. It is about creating opportunities, sharing knowledge, and inspiring others to believe that they belong in the world of innovation.

From classrooms to communities, from writing my first programs to leading technology initiatives, every experience has shaped the engineer and leader I am becoming.

Creating Impact

SheCanCode BW 🌸

Technology is not only about writing code. It is about creating opportunities.

As the Founder and President of SheCanCode BW, I lead a youth-driven initiative focused on empowering young people, especially girls, to explore technology, coding, and innovation.

Through workshops, mentorship, technology talks, and community programs, we aim to create spaces where young minds can learn, build confidence, and discover their potential in STEM.

Together with my team, we are building a community where the next generation of innovators can dream bigger.
